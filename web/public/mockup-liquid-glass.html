FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this a LinkedIn replacement? +

No. LinkedIn is your network of record. HireGEN is your proof of capability. Keep both — we even let you sync.

How do you verify skills? +

Adaptive 15-min challenges, project review by AI + peer signal, and verified contributions from open-source / repos. Each skill carries a confidence score and decay date.

What does it cost? +

Free for candidates, forever. Companies pay per accepted intro (₹2k–₹10k) or via enterprise plans. No résumé database resale, ever.

When is it launching? +

Closed beta — Q3 2026. Public launch — late 2026. Join the waitlist above to be in the first cohort.
