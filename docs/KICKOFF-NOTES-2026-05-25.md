## 8. HireGEN Specific Positioning

Problem:

> Resumes are noisy, AI-generated, and weak hiring signals. Recruiters waste time filtering candidates, while Tier-2/3 talent remains invisible on network-biased platforms.

Solution:

> HireGEN turns candidate evidence into verifiable skill graphs. It analyzes resumes, GitHub portfolios, projects, and validation tasks, then shows recruiters proof-backed candidates ranked by real capability.

Uniqueness:

- Not a job portal
- Not a resume writer
- Not just an AI dashboard
- Proof-first hiring layer
- Candidate badges linked to evidence
- Git/project-backed skill graph
- Validation lab / anti-cheat policies
- India-first Tier-2/3 talent discovery
