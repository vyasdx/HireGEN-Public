# HireGEN — Product Vision

> Source-of-truth document for *why* this product exists, *who* it's for, and *what win looks like*.

**Last updated:** 2026-05-15

---

## One-line vision

> **The fastest path from skill → job.**

---

## The thesis

LinkedIn is a 22-year-old social network that became the world's professional identity layer. It does that job well. But it was built before:

- Generative AI made traditional resumes meaningless (anyone can generate a "perfect" one).
- India produced 1.5M engineering graduates per year, 70% of whom come from institutions LinkedIn's network effect under-serves.
- Skill half-lives collapsed to 18 months in software, making static credentials misleading.
- Recruiter workflows were drowning in applicant inflation (500 apps for a single posting).

**LinkedIn cannot reinvent itself into a hiring engine without breaking the social network it already is.** That gap is the opening.

---

## Positioning statement

> **For** Indian job seekers and the companies that hire them,
> **HireGEN is** a skill-validated hiring platform
> **that** turns proof of capability — not paper credentials — into the basis of every match.
> **Unlike** LinkedIn, which optimizes for network and identity,
> **HireGEN** optimizes for hiring outcomes.

## What we believe (and others don't)

| The bet | What we believe | What most others believe |
|---|---|---|
| **On credentials** | Resumes are dead. Proof wins. | Polished resumes still signal quality. |
| **On networks** | Network is a barrier, not a feature, for Tier-2/3 talent. | More connections = more opportunity. |
| **On AI in hiring** | AI should rank candidates by proof, not parse resumes for keywords. | AI = better ATS keyword matching. |
| **On candidates** | They should pay $0, forever. | Premium tiers for candidates are fair. |
| **On recruiters** | Pay only on accepted intros. | Subscriptions and résumé databases. |
| **On India** | Tier-2/3 talent is the largest under-monetized supply pool. | Bangalore + Hyderabad + IITs is the market. |

## Target users

### Primary candidate persona — "Priya the Proof-Builder"
- 22–32, Tier-2/3 city or non-IIT background
- Real skills (1–4 yrs experience), but invisible on LinkedIn because the network is hometown-biased
- Has projects, GitHub, side work — all of which today goes into the resume void
- Frustrated by "apply to 100, hear back from 2"
- Will trade 60 minutes of skill validation for a 10x better hit rate

### Primary recruiter persona — "Ravi the Tech Hiring Lead"
- Series B–D tech company in Bangalore / Hyderabad / Mumbai / Pune
- Hires 30–200 engineers/year
- Drowns in unfiltered LinkedIn Recruiter results and Naukri spam
- Will pay ₹5,000 per accepted intro if the top 10 are actually qualified
- Cares about time-to-hire and 6-month retention more than vanity metrics

### Secondary persona — "Fresh-graduate fast-track"
- Final year / 0-exp grad
- IIT/NIT pedigree is *not* their advantage
- Needs a way to show "I built this" beyond projects on GitHub
- Bonus user — not the primary acquisition target at MVP

## The win condition (1-year)

By **May 2027**, HireGEN has:
- ≥ 25,000 candidate proof profiles (skill-validated, not just signed up)
- ≥ 200 paying recruiter accounts
- ≥ 1,000 paid accepted intros
- ≥ 2 marquee customer testimonials (Series C+ Indian tech companies)
- ≥ 1 measured "time-to-hire" customer outcome ≤ 14 days (vs. industry 42)
- Net Revenue Retention > 110% on recruiter side

---

## The win condition (3-year)

By **2029**, HireGEN is:
- The default skill-validation layer for Indian tech hiring (mentioned alongside LinkedIn in JDs).
- ₹50–₹100 cr ARR run-rate.
- Acquired by Naukri / LinkedIn / Indeed at a **product** multiple ($50M–$200M range), OR independent with $20M+ Series B.

## What we will NOT build

This list is as important as the feature list. Saying no protects the wedge.

- ❌ Social feed / posts / influencer content
- ❌ Messaging / DM inbox
- ❌ Browsable job board (matching only, no spray-and-pray)
- ❌ Group / community features
- ❌ Mobile app at launch (responsive web only)
- ❌ Course marketplace (affiliate links to existing EdTechs only)
- ❌ Résumé generator / "AI resume writer" (we kill resumes, not enable them)
- ❌ Premium tier for candidates (free forever — moat)
- ❌ Selling user data, ever (moat)

## Relationship to the broader portfolio

HireGEN is a sibling to other ideas in the same portfolio:

- **Layoff Forensics** (Tier-3 ideation) — diagnostic + prescription for laid-off workers. Naturally funnels its "prescription" users into HireGEN's skill validation flow. Shared skill graph, shared recruiter network.
- **VidyaSutra** — career advisory layer. Philosophical sibling.
- **AI-AppLens** — same multi-agent orchestration craft, different domain.

When/if Layoff Forensics ships, the integration is: *"You were laid off → here's why → here's the skill gap → validate it on HireGEN → get matched."* End-to-end loop, two products, one customer.
