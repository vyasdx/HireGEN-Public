FAQ

What this is, and what it is not.

Is HireGEN a LinkedIn replacement?

No. LinkedIn remains identity and network. HireGEN is the proof and matching layer that can sit beside it.

What makes this hard to copy?

The defensible part is not the landing page or AI parsing. It is the validation rubric, recruiter trust, candidate supply, and outcome data loop.

Where should the MVP start?

A narrow segment: Indian frontend or full-stack engineers with 1-4 years of experience, manually curated until the quality bar is obvious.
